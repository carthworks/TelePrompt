Welcome to MyTeleprompter!

This is a professional teleprompter application designed to help you deliver flawless speeches, presentations, and videos.

Key Features:
• Smooth scrolling with adjustable speed
• Multiple theme options (Dark, Light, Sepia, Blue)
• Customizable font sizes
• Fullscreen mode for distraction-free reading
• Keyboard shortcuts for easy control
• Bookmark support to mark important sections
• Wake lock to keep your screen on

How to Use:
1. Upload your speech text file or paste your content
2. Adjust the speed and font size to your preference
3. Press Play or use the spacebar to start scrolling
4. Use arrow keys to control speed during playback
5. Press F for fullscreen mode
